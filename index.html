SPIRITUAL ACTIVITIES IN VERNACULAR

  • Holy Mass - First Sunday of the month in Church Chapel at 7.30 p.m.

  • Holy Mass followed with Holy Rosary - Forth Sunday of the month in Church Mini Hall ( Bishop Paul Hall ) at 4.15 p.m.

  • Holy hour Adoration - Third Friday of the month in Church Mini Hall ( Bishop Paul Hall ) at 7.30 p.m.

  • Praise and Worship Prayer Service - Every Sunday in room #05 at 4.15 p.m. ( Except forth Sunday ).

  • Intercessory Prayer Service - Third Wednesday in room #03 at 8.00 p.m.

  • Bible Studies - Every Sunday in room #03 at 10.00 a.m.

  • Holy Rosary Devotion - In the months of May and October, chain rosary is recited during the whole month.
